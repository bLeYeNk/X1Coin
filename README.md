# ATS Coin

## Executive Summary

### Introduction
ATS Coin aims to revolutionize international transactions by providing a decentralized, secure, and standardized blockchain solution. Each of the 195 participating countries is allocated a unique token based on its country code, with a fixed total supply of 100,000,000 tokens per country. ATS Coin serves as the foundational mechanism for cross-border transactions, offering a secure and efficient platform. ATS Coin has a global maximum supply of 21,000,000 ATS Coins.

### Business Objectives
1. **Global Adoption:** Establish ATS Coin as the preferred blockchain for international transactions.
2. **Country-specific Tokens:** Allocate a total supply of 100,000,000 tokens per country.
3. **Global Maximum Supply:** Maintain a global maximum supply of 21,000,000 ATS Coins.
4. 3. **Sectors:** Allocate a total supply of 100,000,000 tokens per sector.

- **ATS (Alliance Token Standard)**

  ## Objectives

1. **Tokenized Sectors**
   - Create distinct tokens for major global sectors, such as Technology, Healthcare, and Energy.
   - Allocate a maximum supply of 100,000,000 tokens for each sector.
   - Implement smart contracts to facilitate sector-specific transactions and governance.

2. **Tokenized Countries**
   - Introduce individual tokens for different countries, providing a blockchain representation for their economic activities.
   - Set a maximum supply of 100,000,000 tokens for each country.
   - Implement blockchain-based governance models for country-specific decisions.

3. **ATS as the Utility Coin**
   - Design and deploy the ATS utility coin to act as the common currency across sectors and countries.
   - Enable seamless transactions, governance, and access to various features within the DEX using ATS.
   - Ensure interoperability and frictionless exchange between sectors and countries.

4. **Blockchain Infrastructure**
   - Utilize the SHA-256 blockchain for its security, immutability, and decentralization features.
   - Implement smart contracts to automate and enforce sector and country-specific rules.
   - Ensure scalability and efficiency in handling a diverse range of transactions.

5. **Community Engagement**
   - Foster a vibrant and engaged community around the DEX project.
   - Encourage participation in governance through decentralized decision-making processes.
   - Facilitate feedback mechanisms to improve and evolve the DEX based on user input.
